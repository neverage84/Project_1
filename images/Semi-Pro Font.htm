Semi-Pro Font

About Semi-Pro Font

Semi-Pro is a 2008 American sports film that tells the story of Jackie Moon, a fictional 1970s ABA player who works to get his team into the NBA when the leagues merge.

The font used for the film title on the poster is very similar to Collegiate Black FLF by Casady & Greene, or Freshman by William Boyd, but the accented letters are missing from both fonts. The fonts used for the team wordmark (Flint Tropics) on the jersey are Street Van Haulnhouse Solid from House Industries and an unidentified rounded sans serif.

If you do not want to download and install the font but just like to create simple text or logos using Semi-Pro Font, just use the text generator below.
